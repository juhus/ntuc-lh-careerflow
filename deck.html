The Solution

One integrated platform. Four connected modules.

CareerFlow automates the full trainee lifecycle — from enrolment through verified employment and SSG compliance — on a single, PDPA-ready platform purpose-built for workforce development.

1Smart Enrolment

AI assessment analyses backgrounds, identifies transferable skills, and recommends courses with confidence scores — replacing subjective evaluations with data-driven decisions.

2Trainee Tracker

Real-time pipeline visibility across the full journey, with automated document reminders and self-service upload for employment evidence.

3Placement Engine

Skills-to-job matching ranked by fit score, with proactive at-risk alerts when placements stall or employer engagement drops.

4Analytics & Compliance

Live KPI dashboard, automated SSG reports, PDPA consent management, and comprehensive audit trails.
